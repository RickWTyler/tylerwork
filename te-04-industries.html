Industries served

The work has gone where the work was needed.

Tyler Ergonomics has served organizations across a wide range of sectors. The methodology stays the same — credentialed assessment, written findings, prioritized recommendations. The context changes; the standard doesn't.

Healthcare

Cancer center workstation design and facility layout consultation, operating room ergonomics developed with surgical teams, and workstation support for nursing, radiology, and administrative staff — across hospitals and specialty centers.

Oil & Gas

Office and technical facility workstations, control room ergonomics, return-to-work ergonomic support for office staff, and remote evaluations for energy-sector staff across distributed operations. Tyler Ergonomics has anchored its energy-sector work in this industry since 2002.

Universities

Workstation assessments for faculty, research, and administrative staff. Return-to-work support for academic personnel. Student ergonomics support through the Life|After® Back to Class program. Engagement experience across multiple Louisiana institutions.

K-12 & School Districts

District-level workstation assessment, administrator and teacher RTW support, and ergonomic training for facility staff.

Government & Municipal

Office and field staff assessments, accommodation support, and return-to-work programs for state and local employers.

Insurance Carriers

Workplace ergonomic engagements for office-based insurance staff — claims processing, customer service, and administrative roles. Engagement experience includes Louisiana-based health plan administration.

Film & Television

Workstation ergonomics for post-production editors — long-duration seated work, multi-monitor setups, and the physical demands of editing schedules.

Athletic Conferences

Office ergonomics for conference administrative staff, coaches, and athletic department personnel.

Architecture Firms

Drafting station and workstation design for design professionals — accommodating both digital and traditional drafting workflows.

Restaurants

Front-of-house and back-of-house ergonomics, prep station design, and physical-demand accommodation for service industry staff.

Technology & Remote Workforce

Home office evaluations, distributed-workforce ergonomic programs, and virtual training for companies with hybrid or fully remote teams.
