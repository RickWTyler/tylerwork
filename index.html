How it works

Simple to start. Thorough in practice.

Whether you're an individual booking a home office evaluation or an employer engaging for a full workplace assessment program, the process is straightforward.

1

Book or inquire

Individuals book and pay online for a fixed-price home office evaluation. Employers submit an inquiry and receive a scoped proposal within two business days.

2

Assessment

On-site visit or virtual session led by a credentialed specialist. Assessment is structured, thorough, and documented — not a checklist exercise.

3

Written report

Every engagement produces a written report with specific findings and prioritized recommendations — something you can act on, share with HR or a supervisor, and reference over time.

4

Follow-up

Home office evaluations include a 30-day follow-up. Employer engagements include a review session to address implementation questions and confirm recommendations are in place.
